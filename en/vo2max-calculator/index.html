Methodology: Rockport Walking Test

Equation

VO₂max (ml/kg/min) = 132.853
                      - (0.0769 × Weight in lbs)
                      - (0.3877 × Age in years)
                      + (6.315 × Sex) [1 = male, 0 = female]
                      - (3.2649 × Time in minutes)
                      - (0.1565 × Heart Rate in bpm)

Validation:
  - Kline et al., Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise (1987)
  - Correlation with lab-measured VO₂max: r = 0.88-0.93
  - Standard error: ±5 ml/kg/min

Test Protocol

  1. Preparation: Avoid vigorous activity 24 hours before test; well-hydrated
  2. Warm-up: 5-10 minutes easy walking
  3. Test: Walk 1 mile (1.61 km) as fast as possible while maintaining walking gait (no running)
  4. Measurement: Record time (to nearest second) and heart rate immediately upon finishing
  5. Track: Flat 400m track (4 laps) or measured 1-mile route
