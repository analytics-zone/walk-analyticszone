About Walk Analytics

Science-based walking performance tracking, built by walkers for walkers

Our Mission

Walk Analytics brings professional-grade performance tracking to every walker. We believe that advanced metrics like Walking Zones, Gait Analysis, and Health Metrics shouldn't be locked behind expensive platforms or require complex coaching software.

Meet the Developer

Albert Arnó

Creator

Albert is a software developer and former competitive walker with over 15 years of Masters walking experience. After struggling to find affordable, science-based run analytics tools that weren't tied to specific hardware or cloud platforms, he created Walk Analytics.

Why Walk Analytics Was Created:

"I was frustrated by expensive subscriptions, vendor lock-in requiring specific devices, and lack of control over my own data. I wanted a tool that calculated walking zones and gait metrics correctly, worked with any Apple Health-compatible device, and kept my data private. When I couldn't find it, I built it."

Our Principles

Editorial Standards

All metrics and formulas on Walk Analytics and this website are based on peer-reviewed sports science research. We cite original sources and provide transparent calculations.
