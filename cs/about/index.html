About Walk Analytics

Science-based walking performance tracking, built by walkers for walkers

Our Mission

Walk Analytics brings professional-grade performance tracking to every walker. We believe that advanced metrics like Walking Zones, Gait Analysis, and Health Metrics shouldn't be locked behind expensive platforms or require complex coaching software.

Our Principles

  • Science First: All metrics based on peer-reviewed research. We cite our sources and show our formulas.
  • Privacy by Design: 100% local data processing. No servers, no accounts, no tracking. You own your data.
  • Platform Agnostic: Works with any Apple Health compatible device. No vendor lock-in.
  • Transparency: Open formulas, clear calculations, honest limitations. No black box algorithms.
  • Accessibility: Advanced metrics shouldn't require a degree in sports science. We explain concepts clearly.

Scientific Foundation

Walk Analytics is built on decades of peer-reviewed sports science research:

Walking Zones

Based on heart rate and pace research for walking. Walking zones help optimize training intensity for health, fitness, and performance goals.

Key Research: Heart rate zone training adapted for walking biomechanics and energy systems.

Gait Analysis

Stride length, cadence, and walking efficiency metrics. Used by physical therapists and coaches to track movement quality and prevent injury.

Standard Metrics: Stride Rate, Distance Per Stride, and Walking Efficiency. Lower efficiency scores indicate better performance.

Health Metrics

VO2max estimation, calorie expenditure, and cardiovascular health indicators. Tracks overall fitness improvements over time.

Implementation: Apple Health integration for comprehensive health tracking and trend analysis.

Walking Efficiency

Efficiency metrics combining time and stride count. Used to track technical improvements and optimize walking form.

Standard Metrics: Walking Efficiency = Time + Strides. Lower scores indicate better efficiency.
