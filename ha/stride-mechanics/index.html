Makanikan Tafiya na Tafiya: Cadence, Tsawon Tsayin, da Lokacin Gait

Jagoran tsakiya don fahimtar hanyar tafiya, fassarar nazarin gait, saitin yankunan tafiya, da kuma yanke shawarar abin da zai inganta na gaba

Amsa da sauri

Makanikan tafiyar tafiya bayyana yadda kuke tsara kowane mataki: ƙaranci, tsayin mataki da tsayin daka, tuntuɓar ƙasa, tallafi biyu, da daidaitawa. Ga yawancin masu tafiya, makasudin aiki ba tsayin daka ba ne. Yana da a abin maimaitawa wanda ya dace da aikin, yana kiyaye tasiri mai ma'ana, kuma yana goyan bayan ƙarfin da ya dace.

  • Mafi amfani: Fassarar hanyar tafiya, tafiya cikin gaggauce, da kuma gano alamu marasa inganci kafin su zama halaye
  • Mafi amfani anchors: ƙwaƙƙwalwa, saurin tafiya, da kuma sauƙin duban ƙima
  • Babban kuskure: tilasta matakai masu tsayi a maimakon inganta haɓaka, matsayi, da lokacin turawa
  • Mafi kyawun mataki na gaba: haɗa form zuwa nazarin gait, yankunan tafiya, kuma WSS maimakon a yi hukunci da lamba daya a ware

Mahimman Abubuwan Da Ya Kamata A Dauka

  • Cadence yawanci shine mafi tsafta lever don canza ƙarfin tafiya ba tare da wuce gona da iri ba
  • Tsawon tafiya ya kamata yayi girma daga mafi kyawun matsayi, turawa, da motsin hip, ba tare da kaiwa gaba ba
  • Tallafi biyu yana taimakawa bayyana taka tsantsan, iyakoki ma'auni, da dalilin da yasa masu tafiya biyu a gudu ɗaya zasu iya bambanta sosai
  • Symmetry da lokaci al'amari fiye da lokacin da kake kula da zafi, farfadowa, ko ingancin motsi fiye da lokacin da kake kula da matakai kawai
  • Tarin gaske:nazarin gait, yankunan tafiya, gait yadda ya dace, kimar tafiya, kuma WSS

Yawancin masu tafiya ba sa buƙatar haddace kowane lokaci na tafiya. Suna buƙatar sanin waɗanne injiniyoyi masu tafiya ne ke da mahimmanci ga burin da ke gabansu: tafiya don lafiya, motsawa da sauri tare da tsari mai kyau, sake ginawa bayan koma baya, ko fahimtar dalilin da yasa yanayin smartwatch ya canza.

Tambayoyi masu amfani suna da amfani:

  • Shin ina tafiya a hankali saboda ƙarancin ɗan adam, saboda matakan gajeru ne, ko kuma duka biyun suna da iyaka?
  • Shin wannan tsari ne na taka tsantsan na yau da kullun, ko alamar cewa daidaito, zafi, ko gajiya yana canza tafiyata?
  • Shin zan inganta ƙarfi ta hanyar haɓaka iyawa, ta canza ƙasa, ko ta tilasta matakai masu tsayi?
  • Wane shafi zan yi amfani da shi na gaba: bincike, yankuna, inganci, kimantawa, ko nauyin horo?

Abin da Stride Mechanics A zahiri Aunawa

Tsara makanikai su ne tsarin mataki, ba maki na motsa jiki ba. Suna gaya muku yadda aka tsara motsi ta hanyar sadarwa, tallafi, turawa, da lilo. Lokacin da waɗancan makanikan suka kasance cikin haɗin kai, tafiya yana jin daɗi da arziƙi. Lokacin da suka yi nisa, gudu, kwanciyar hankali, da kwanciyar hankali yawanci suna yawo tare da su.

Samfurin Hankali Mai Amfani

Ka yi tunani kadence as rhythm, tsayin tafiya azaman ɗaukar hoto, goyon baya biyu a matsayin taka tsantsan da kwanciyar hankali, kuma daidaito a matsayin ma'aunin motsi. Tare suna bayyana dalilin da yasa tafiya ke jin santsi, da wuya, ko rashin tausayi.

Inda Stride Makanikai ke Taimakawa Mafi Girma

Tafiya Don Lafiya

Yi amfani da tsattsauran ra'ayi da saurin tafiya don tabbatar da cewa "tafiya" a haƙiƙa tana kai matsakaicin ƙarfi lokacin da wannan shine manufar. Sannan amfani yankunan tafiya don tsara ƙoƙari.

Ingancin motsi

Yi amfani da tambari, tsayin mataki, da goyon baya biyu don lura da tsarin kariya, gajiya, ko diyya kafin su zama tsayayyen tafiyarku.

Bayan-Haye Ko Nazari na Kullum

Amfani nazarin gait lokacin da kuke buƙatar fassarar, ba kawai albarkatun HealthKit kawai ba. Aikin shine fassara bayanai zuwa yanke shawara.

Load ɗin horo

Yi amfani da ma'aunin tsari don zaɓar nau'in zaman da ya dace, sannan amfani WSS don kwatanta damuwa horo na waɗannan tafiya akan lokaci.

Dogaro mai mahimmanci: Saurin tafiya ba koyaushe ya fi kyau tafiya ba. Idan cadence ya tashi amma matsayi ya rushe, matakai sun kai nisa gaba, ko asymmetry ya girma, kuna iya zama ingancin ciniki don taki.

The Core Mechanics To Watch

Zagayen Tafiya

Cikakken zagayowar tafiya yana gudana daga bugun diddige ɗaya zuwa bugun diddige na gaba na ƙafa ɗaya. Ba kamar gudu ba, tafiya yana ci gaba da tuntuɓar ƙasa kuma ya haɗa da a mataki na goyon baya biyu inda ƙafafu biyu suka raba kaya a taƙaice.

Cadence da Ƙarfi

Ga mafi yawan masu tafiya, ƙwaƙƙwaran ita ce hanya mafi sauƙi don auna ƙarfi. Bincike yana tallafawa koyaushe kusan matakai 100 a cikin minti daya a matsayin ƙofa mai amfani don matsakaicin ƙarfin tafiya a cikin manya da yawa.

Tsawon Mataki vs Tsawon Tsayi

Tsawon mataki shi ne diddige-zuwa diddige daga ƙafa ɗaya zuwa wancan. Tsawon tafiya matakai biyu ne: bugun diddige zuwa bugun diddige na gaba na ƙafa ɗaya. Gudu shine samfurin ƙwaƙƙwalwa da tsayin tafiya, amma wannan baya nufin yakamata ku tilasta tsayin tsayin da hannu.

  • Kyakkyawan tsayin tafiya yawanci yakan zo daga matsayi, tsawo na hip, da tsaftataccen turawa
  • wuce gona da iri yana faruwa ne lokacin da ƙafar ƙafa ta yi nisa a gaban tsakiyar taro
  • Yawan wuce gona da iri yana kama da sauri amma yawanci yana ƙara ƙarfin birki da ɓarna makamashi
  • Ga mafi yawan masu tafiya, ƙaramin ƙarar ƙarar ya fi aminci fiye da ƙoƙarin isa gaba

Taimako Biyu Da Tsarkake Gait

Tallafi sau biyu yana tashi lokacin da kake son ƙarin kwanciyar hankali. Wannan na iya zama al'ada a kan ƙasa mara daidaituwa, yayin ɗaukar kaya, ko lokacin tafiya cikin taka tsantsan. Yana zama mafi amfani azaman alamar faɗakarwa lokacin da ta tashi tare da saurin tafiya a hankali, gajeriyar matakai, ko bayyanannen asymmetry.

Symmetry da Lokaci

Ƙananan bambance-bambancen hagu-dama na al'ada ne. Abin da ke da mahimmanci shine ko asymmetry yana da ƙarfi, ingantawa, ko faɗaɗa akan lokaci. Alamar alama ta zama mafi mahimmanci lokacin da kake gyara matsala, sarrafa zafi, ko kwatanta ingancin motsi cikin makonni.

Me Yasa Wadannan Misalan Suna da Muhimmanci

Gudun tafiya iri ɗaya na iya zuwa daga injiniyoyi daban-daban. Ɗayan tsari na iya zama santsi da tattalin arziki. Wani kuma na iya zama mai hankali, asymmetrical, ko rashin inganci. Ƙwararrun injiniyoyi suna taimakawa bayyana bambanci kafin ku yanke shawarar abin da za ku horar da su.

Tambayoyin da ake yawan yi

Shin zan yi ƙoƙarin ƙara tsayin tafiya don tafiya da sauri?

Yawancin lokaci a'a. Yawancin masu yawo suna yin mafi kyau ta hanyar haɓaka ƙima, matsayi, da ingancin turawa. Tilasta dogon mataki sau da yawa yakan juya zuwa wuce gona da iri da karin birki.

Wane irin ƙwaƙƙwaran ƙaƙƙarfan tafiya ne?

Ga manya da yawa, kusan matakai 100 a cikin minti ɗaya alama ce mai matsakaicin ƙarfi. Halin mutum ɗaya har yanzu yana da mahimmanci, amma tunani ne mai fa'ida.

Me yasa tallafi biyu ke da mahimmanci don tafiya?

Yana nuna tsawon lokacin da kuke ajiye ƙafafu biyu a ƙasa don kwanciyar hankali. Maɗaukakin dabi'u na iya zama na al'ada akan ƙasa maras kyau, amma kuma suna iya yin sigina mafi tsayayyen tsarin tafiya idan an haɗa su da saurin gudu ko asymmetry.

Yaushe zan fi kula da siminti?

Alamar alama tana da mahimmanci lokacin da kuke fama da ciwo, rauni na baya-bayan nan, gyarawa, ko ingantaccen canji na ingancin motsi. Don tafiya ta yau da kullun, ƙananan bambance-bambancen hagu-dama na al'ada ne.

Menene mafi kyawun shafin aboki bayan wannan?

Idan kuna son fassarar, karanta nazarin gait. Idan kuna son makasudin zaman, karanta yankunan tafiya. Idan kana son saurin duba kai, yi amfani kimar tafiya.
